Meet Our Attorney

Aderibigbe Zainab Yetunde

Founder & Principal Attorney

Aderibigbe Zainab Yetunde is a licensed Canadian immigration lawyer and the founding partner of ZYA Law Firm. With over a decade of experience in Canadian immigration law, she has helped hundreds of individuals and families successfully navigate the complex Canadian immigration system.

Her practice focuses on providing personalized, culturally informed legal representation to clients from diverse backgrounds, with a particular commitment to serving the Nigerian and broader African diaspora communities in Canada.

Our Values

What We Stand For

At ZYA Law Firm, our work is guided by core principles that ensure every client receives the highest standard of legal service.

Compassion

We understand that immigration is deeply personal. Every client is treated with empathy, respect, and genuine care for their unique circumstances.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our dealings. Honest assessments, transparent communication, and trustworthy advice are our hallmarks.

Our Journey

The ZYA Story

Early Beginnings

2017 - 2021

Aderibigbe Zainab Yetunde completed her legal education at the University of ABUAD in Nigeria, followed by her LL.M at the University of Toronto, specializing in immigration and refugee law.

Called to the Bar

2022

Admitted to the Law Society of Ontario, beginning her practice at a prestigious Toronto immigration law firm where she honed her expertise in complex immigration cases.

Founding ZYA Law Firm

2025

Driven by a vision to provide more personalized, culturally attuned legal services, Zainab founded ZYA Law Firm in Scarborough, Ontario, serving clients from across the globe.

Growing Impact

Today, ZYA Law Firm can help clients achieve their Canadian immigration dreams. From Express Entry to family sponsorship, deportation defense to citizenship, we continue to expand our impact.

What We Do

How We Protect & Serve Immigrants

At ZYA Law, we don't just file paperwork — we safeguard your future. Every service is delivered with your safety, dignity, and success as our absolute priority.

Legal Protection

We provide full legal representation before IRCC, the Immigration Refugee Board, and Canadian courts. From deportation defense to admissibility hearings, we stand between you and removal.

Immigrant Safety Advocacy

We prioritize your physical and legal safety throughout the immigration process. Confidential consultations, secure document handling, and protection against exploitation or fraud.

Application Excellence

Every application is prepared with meticulous attention to detail. We ensure accuracy, completeness, and compliance — because one mistake can cost you your future in Canada.

Family Reunification

We help families stay together. Spousal sponsorships, parent sponsorships, dependent applications — we fight to keep your loved ones by your side.

Student Pathway Guidance

We've helped 200+ international students secure study permits and transition to permanent residence. From school selection to PGWP to PR — we guide the entire journey.

Humanitarian Support

For those facing persecution, violence, or impossible circumstances in their home countries, we provide compassionate legal pathways including asylum claims and H&C applications.

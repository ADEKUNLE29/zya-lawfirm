Work Visas & Permits

Canada's economy relies on skilled workers from around the world. Whether you're a tech professional, healthcare worker, tradesperson, or entrepreneur, we help you navigate the complex work permit landscape to build your career in Canada legally and safely.

LMIA-based work permits (employer-sponsored)
Intra-company transfers (L-1 equivalent)
Open work permits (spousal, bridging, PGWP)
Post-Graduation Work Permits (PGWP)
International Experience Canada (IEC)
Global Talent Stream (fast-track for tech workers)

Your Safety: We verify employer legitimacy, ensure contract fairness, and protect you from exploitation or fraudulent job offers that could jeopardize your status.

Initial study permit applications
Study permit extensions & renewals
Co-op work permit applications
Study-to-PR pathway planning
Visitor visa to study permit transitions
Refused study permit appeals

03

Family Sponsorship

Family is everything. Canadian immigration law recognizes this fundamental truth, and so do we. We help Canadian citizens and permanent residents reunite with their loved ones through every available family sponsorship pathway.

Spousal & common-law partner sponsorship
Parent & grandparent sponsorship (PGP)
Dependent child sponsorship
Super Visa for extended family visits
Orphaned relative & adopted child sponsorship
Lonely Canadian & remaining relative applications

Your Safety: We protect vulnerable family members from exploitation, ensure financial sponsorship requirements are met, and safeguard against marriage fraud allegations that could destroy families.

Reunite Your Family

04

Express Entry & Permanent Residence

Express Entry is Canada's flagship immigration system for skilled workers — but it's also one of the most competitive. With our expertise in CRS score optimization and Provincial Nominee Programs, we maximize your chances of receiving that coveted Invitation to Apply (ITA).

Express Entry profile creation & optimization
Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)
Canadian Experience Class (CEC)
Federal Skilled Worker (FSW) applications
Federal Skilled Trades (FST) programs
CRS score improvement strategies

Your Safety: We ensure complete accuracy in your Express Entry profile, protect against misrepresentation charges (which carry a 5-year ban), and guide you through every document requirement.

Start Your PR Journey
PR Pathway Experts
05

Citizenship & Naturalization

Becoming a Canadian citizen is the culmination of your immigration journey — and a moment of profound pride. We guide you through the entire citizenship process, from eligibility assessment to the oath ceremony, ensuring every requirement is met with precision.

Citizenship application preparation & filing
Citizenship test preparation & coaching
Physical presence calculation & optimization
Residency obligation appeals
Citizenship by descent applications
Renunciation & restoration of citizenship

Your Safety: We protect your permanent resident status during the citizenship process, ensure tax compliance doesn't jeopardize your application, and safeguard against criminal inadmissibility issues.

06

Deportation Defense & Appeals

When deportation threatens everything you've built, you need a fighter in your corner. We've successfully defended over 50 families from removal, turning seemingly hopeless cases into victories. If you're facing a removal order, inadmissibility finding, or detention — we are your last line of defense.

Removal order appeals (IAD & Federal Court)
Admissibility hearings & defense
Detention reviews & bail hearings
Humanitarian & Compassionate (H&C) applications
Pre-Removal Risk Assessments (PRRA)
Criminal rehabilitation & record suspensions

URGENT: Deportation cases are time-sensitive. Every day matters. Contact us immediately if you or a loved one has received a removal order or detention notice. We offer emergency consultations.

Your Safety: We provide emergency legal protection, coordinate with detention centers, and fight for your right to remain in Canada. Your physical safety and family unity are our absolute priority.

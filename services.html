What We Offer

Immigration Solutions for Every Pathway

From temporary visas to permanent residency and citizenship, we provide expert guidance through every stage of the Canadian immigration process.

Work & Business

Work Visas & Permits

Navigate the Canadian labour market with confidence. We assist with Labour Market Impact Assessments (LMIA), intra-company transfers, open work permits, and specialized work authorizations for professionals, entrepreneurs, and business owners.

  • Intra-company transfers
  • Post-graduation work permits
  • Spousal open work permits
